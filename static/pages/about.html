About Exponential

Exponential Software is the evolving identity behind a decade of creative and technical exploration, originally known as MightyMawse Games, then Mawse Software.

What began in 2013 with a childhood curiosity for Scratch has grown into a deep passion for software development, game design, and everything in between.

This site serves as a central portfolio for the projects I've built over the years—ranging from experimental games to full-scale applications. Exponential Software is also available for freelance or collaborative software development work.
